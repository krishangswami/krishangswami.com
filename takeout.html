TAKE-OUT BOX // PACKAGING DESIGN
The aim of this project was to design a Take-Out Box for food.

As a class project, the box had many restrictions;
"You may use up to 3 separate pieces of E-flute cardboard. All pieces must be able to come from a single 18 x 24" sheet. No fasteners (nails, screws, etc.) or glue may be used. Tabs and slots should be used to connect sections within the design."
My final design encoprated tabs and was made from one piece of cardboard. It had a triangular shape and a flat back for easy holding. These shapes added to the stability, strength and grip of the box.
