robin
driving experience

Robin is a voice focused human to computer interaction system. This is a team project done with Peter Franko, Stella Ding, Oscar Marczyński and Johnny Knutsen.

We designed it in ways to remove as many distractions as possible for the driver of the car. Our design revolves around the navigation system.

Through augmented reality glasses we show the driver the directions as if they were on the road and all the interactions with the system is done through voice. This removes the need to reach out to touch or look away from the road.
