About Me

Hi, I am an Interaction Design student at the University of Washington.

My journey so far has been interesting to say the least. Growing up in India and studying at an international boarding school exposed me to many cultures and a rich variety of experiences. I learned to appreciate novel and diverse perspectives and developed a desire to learn the unfamiliar. Consequently, I explored and decided to major in something versatile; design.

Many aspects of techonlogy have become a fundamental part of being human and emerging technologies are currently shaping our lives. As Designers, our work embodies the future, so, I want to be sure to innovate towards a positive impact by working on meaningful problems and challenging projects.
