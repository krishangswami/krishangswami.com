Description
This is a personal project. The idea of the mobile application is crowd-sourcing data and providing a visualization of the collected data.

We've become a society that consumes, if not thrives on information. Today, we have more information than ever and with a plethora of options, it's easy to discover figures that aren't facts.
Multiple companies today benefit from information collected directly and indirectly from costumers. In a society heavily involved with consumerism, humans themselves are a large source of this data.

Smart phones are capable of measuring many sets of information and so the idea is that Nexus is a mobile application that collects data from a series of connections of cellular phones. It is a central hub where users can view the data visualizations of the data collected from all other users. This makes each phone the primary source of the data.

The design of the app has so far conceptualised data of network strengths. The users can compare the strength of their network providers (eg. Verizon or Sprint) in different areas.
