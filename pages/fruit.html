Public Fruit

Product Design, Digital Commons

2020 • In Progress

This project is an attempt to create a digital commons of personal books.

The product would be an index of the books shared by members and made available for order and reading.
The design challenge here is only in part to conceptualize a product, the other is an exploration of a simple new narrative on ownership and access to books.

/

This concept is primarily inspired by all the books I've received from friends who've extended their private libraries to share books they don't need anymore.

Either through local meetups or the magic of USPS media mail, members can request books based on a currency/barter system.

All the books are 'free' but a persons access would be based on their contribution of books.

At its best, this is an attempt to create a slightly more complex digital iteration of the free neighborhood library boxes.
