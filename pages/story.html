my story

As a child, my Dad would call me the Jack of all trades and a master of none. I have always enjoyed doing many things and my curiosity has lead to me learn and experience a variety of things.

Studying at a boarding school for 11 years with friends coming close to 30 different nationalities, I learned about many cultures and now appreciate all their differences. Over the years, I developed into a person that is intrigued by and enjoys a variety of things.

My diversion from a focus in engineering and my interest in creativity throughout high school, lead me to explore and then major in design. Which I consider as the bridge between all fields.
