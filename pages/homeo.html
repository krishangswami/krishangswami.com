Description
This is a class project done in collaboration with Alexis Bieda and Peter Franko. The project explores modes to reduce consumption of energy and water in the house setting.

With the ultimate goal being to reduce energy and water consumption, the project started by understanding current market solutions. We then interviewed a family to get a grasp of the interaction space to see if we could provide technological assistance to improve the situation and reduce utility costs.

Process

The process then included storyboarding and defining the characteristics of our interaction system with a main focus on reducing the consumers carbon footprint and their utility costs.
