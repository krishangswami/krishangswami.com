2020 • In Progress

Inspired by Indexhibit's model and reasoning to emphasize content over complicated website design I decided to ideate towards a design for standardized consumption of information.
The most basic goal is to provide clarity and access to depth in the information we consume.

This is in response to the general belief that hype seems to the prevelant mode of surfacing knowledge in this age of information and the assumption that this behavior isn't always healthy.
