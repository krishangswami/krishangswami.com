future of breakfast
video project - 2016

The goal was to make two videos about a daily activity and the designed future of that activity.

After ideation and a little bit of thinking, I decided on the activity of making breakfast. I used this oppurtunity to explore my interest in Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things.

The process included a detailed timeline and story boarding. I also had to consider what I would be cooking and how to film it. The storyboard sketches, with yellow to indicate motion, and the videos:

For the second video I showed the future of breakfast. This concept video shows how in the future, humans will give audio instructions to an Artificial Intelligence and implies a connected home with Internet of Things.

I also imagined the process of having breakfast in the morning wouldn't include a human making it.
