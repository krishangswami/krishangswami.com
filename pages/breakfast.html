Description
This is a class project for which I created two videos; one of a daily activity and the other of the designed future of that activity.



After ideating through a couple of ideas, I decided on the activity of making breakfast. I used this oppurtunity to explore my interest in Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things.

The process included a detailed timeline and story boarding. I also had to consider what I would be cooking and how to film it.

Storyboard Sketches
Video One

For the second video I showed the future of breakfast. This concept video shows how in the future, humans will give audio instructions to an Artificial Intelligence and implies a connected home with Internet of Things.

I imagined the process of having breakfast in the morning wouldn't include a human making it and instead focused on what the person would do otherwise while his/her breakfast is getting ready.
