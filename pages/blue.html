2019 -> Present
I joined Ramp as the first designer in June 2019. Ramp is an enterprise facing financial technology company.

Since joining I've iterated on many different parts of the product. For the first 8 months I worked as the only designer to create a design system and 2 different versions of the product to take from private to public launch.

This included constant conversations with engineers to build a source of truth for Ramp's only product.

Now, as the company has grown from 6 to 40+ I mainly focus on owning large chunks of new product functionality and the base design system.
