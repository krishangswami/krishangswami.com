Smart Design

2018. 12 weeks.
UX/UI Design. Data Visualization. Prototyping.



As an Interaction Designer at Smart Design I worked with 2 different clients on many aspects of the projects. Most of my work revolved around solving design issues and proposing researched insights for a large financial institution and designing and researching an ecosystem to mitigate health problems through products for a pharmaceutical company.

My contributions included assisting in interviews, sythesizing data for research, designing user-flows and interfaces, vizualizing collected data and creating usable prototypes.

I also designed and built interactions with Amazon's Alexa to conceptualize a future of healthcare.
